About AJICO

Al Jandool International – AJICO was established in 2007 as an approved & registered construction company from the Central Tender Committee of Kuwait, with an aim to serve the nation professionally in Construction and Contracting Sector.

Since 2007 AJICO continues to strive for providing reliable, efficient & cost-effective services to local authorities and many local and multinational companies on a turnkey basis. We are committed to follow engineering code of practices and standards with HSE at the top, client's ultimate satisfaction.

We have a proven record of accomplishment of past performance defined by a wonderful record of projects completed on time and within budget; often exceeding client and customer expectations.
